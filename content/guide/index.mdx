Welcome to the Ohmega platform! This guide will help you understand what Ohmega is, how it works, and how to get started using it effectively.

## What is Ohmega?

Ohmega is the team created at Utrecht University that made the software for Charge Volt that you are about to use.

## What You'll Learn

In this guide, you'll discover:

- **Basic Concepts** - Understanding the core principles and components of Ohmega
- **Setup Process** - How to configure your environment and get everything working
- **First Steps** - Creating your first project and understanding the workflow
- **Common Tasks** - Day-to-day operations and best practices
- **Troubleshooting** - Solutions to common issues and where to get help

## Getting Started

The best way to learn Ohmega is by doing. We recommend trying things out and if you can't figure it out reference back here.

## Ready to Begin?

Start exploring the sections in the sidebar to begin your journey with Ohmega. We've organized everything to take you from complete beginner to confident user.
