## Left Sidebar

The left sidebar provides quick navigation to other pages:

- **New** - Lets you upload files and create other entities
- **Search** - Open the quick search interface

- **Home** - Returns you to this dashboard
- **Archive** - Lets you search through all uploaded content
- **Tags** - Lets you look through and create new tags
- **Projects** - Allows you to create projects to easily group content
- **Users** - Lets admins manage users

- **Help** - Takes you to the Guide/Documentation page
- **Profile** - Gives you two options on click, Account Settings and Log Out
