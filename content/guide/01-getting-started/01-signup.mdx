## Introduction

By the end of this guide, you'll be able to:

- Successfully create your account using the invitation
- Complete all required registration fields
- Access your new account

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- **An invitation to join** (this is required - you cannot sign up without an invitation)
- Access to the email account where the invitation was sent

<Callout title="Important: Invitation Required">
This is an invite-only platform. You can only sign up if you have received an invitation email. Make sure to use the exact same email address that received the invitation when creating your account.
</Callout>

## Step 1: Access the Sign Up Page

1. Check your email for your invitation 
2. Click the invitation link in the email
3. You'll see the registration form with the Charge event photo

![Sign up page](/images/guides/signup-page.png)
*The sign up page*

## Step 2: Fill in the Form

1. Enter your first and last name
2. **Enter the exact same email address that received the invitation**
3. Enter a strong password, and confirm it
4. Accept Terms and Conditions
5. Click the Sign Up button

## Verification

After successful registration, you should be redirected to the Login page.

## Next Steps

After succesfully creating your account, you'll want to:

- [Log in to your account](./02-login)
- Explore the home page 

## Need Help?

If you run into issues not covered in this guide:

- Contact the person who invited you

## Important Notes

- This application is an exclusive, invite-only platform
- Your invitation is tied to a specific email address and cannot be transferred
